:= FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS =:

Q: How far in advance can I book a discussion room?
A: Bookings open 14 days in advance. You can hold at most 3 active bookings.

Q: What happens if I am late to my booking?
A: If you do not check in within 15 minutes of the start time, the booking is
released automatically and made available to other students.

Q: How long is a lecturer appointment?
A: Appointments are 20 minutes by default and must be requested for a published
consultation slot. Cancel at least 2 hours in advance if you cannot attend.

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Submit a leave application with the start date, end date and a reason. Medical
leave longer than 3 days needs a supporting document reference. You will receive a
leave reference number on submission.

Q: Who do I contact about a booking problem?
A: Email the Campus Services helpdesk at helpdesk@taylors.edu.my or call ext. 1200.

Q: Can I renew a library book?
A: Most books can be renewed twice for 21 days each unless another reader has placed
a reservation on the item.
